Lamborghini GT350

    Details :


  • Model Name: Lamborghini 350 GT
  • Production Years: 1964–1966
  • Engine: 3.5 L V12 (Front-mounted)
  • Units Produced: Approximately 120
  • 0–100 km/h: ~3.6 seconds
  • Body Style: 2-door Coupé (Grand Tourer)
  • Significance: First production vehicle built by Automobili Lamborghini.

Lamborghini Diablo

    Details :


  • Engine: 5.7 L or 6.0 L V12 (Mid-Rear mounted)
  • Power: 492 hp (362 kW)
  • Drivetrain: Initially Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD), with the later VT
  • Top Speed: Over 200 mph (325 km/h) — it was the first production mark.
  • Design Highlight: Maintained the signature scissor doors for the V12 flagship models.
  • Production Years: 1990–2001
